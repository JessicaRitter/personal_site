ReciMe

ReciMe aims to help indecisive foodies that want excellent recipes that take into account their dietary restrictions. Users select the meal to search for and ReciMe displays three recipes filtered by their restrictions. Recipes can be found by meal as well as by tag through the tagging page. Users can rate recipes they've made which are displayed on their profile page so they can always find the recipes they loved again.

Tech Stack: Python/Flask, Bootstrap,AJAX, JQuery,HTML,CSS, Jinja2, Beautiful Soup, Bcrypt, SQLAlchemy, Postgres.
